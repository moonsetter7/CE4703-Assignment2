◆ CardDeck_shuffle()

void CardDeck_shuffle ( CardDeck * deck)

Shuffles a deck following the algorithm outlined in the assignment.

Algorithm:

  1. Take a source deck.
  2. Create a temporary, empty deck.
  3. Get a random number r that has a value between 1 and N, where N is the no. of cards in the source deck.
  4. Remove the r-th card from the source deck and insert it to the beginning of the temporary deck.
  5. Decrement N by one.
  6. Repeat steps 3 to 5 until N equals 0.
Parameters
deckCard deck to be shuffled
